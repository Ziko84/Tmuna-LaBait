The Craft

A single sheet.
A single pass.

Every panel begins as a drawing, refined for weeks before it meets the laser. Cut once from a single sheet, hand-deburred, patinated, and paired with warm 2700K light — never cold, never blue. The result is an object that reads as architecture by day and as a lantern by night.
